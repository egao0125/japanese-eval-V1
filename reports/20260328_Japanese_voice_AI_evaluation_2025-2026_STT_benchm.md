## Recommendations for Japanese Voice AI Evaluation

### Immediate Research Priorities (2025)

1. **Establish Baseline Metrics**: Develop comprehensive CER/WER benchmarks for Japanese ASR in telephony environments
2. **Telephony-Specific Evaluation Frameworks**: Create adapted MOS and intelligibility metrics for Japanese TTS in compressed audio scenarios
3. **Real-World Data Collection**: Gather representative Japanese telephony conversation datasets for evaluation purposes

### Medium-Term Development Goals (2025-2026)

1. **Standardization Initiatives**: Collaborate with Japanese telecommunications standards bodies to establish evaluation protocols
2. **Cross-Platform Benchmarking**: Develop evaluation frameworks that account for various telephony technologies (VoIP, cellular, landline)
3. **Cultural Adaptation**: Incorporate Japanese-specific linguistic and cultural factors into evaluation metrics

### Long-Term Strategic Objectives (2026+)

1. **Regulatory Compliance Framework**: Align evaluation metrics with anticipated Japanese regulatory requirements
2. **Industry Adoption Programs**: Facilitate widespread adoption of standardized evaluation practices
3. **Continuous Improvement Mechanisms**: Establish feedback loops between evaluation results and model development

### Critical Research Gaps Requiring Attention

- **Latency Impact Analysis**: Quantify the relationship between system latency and user experience in Japanese telephony AI
- **Code-Switching Evaluation**: Develop metrics for Japanese-English code-switching scenarios common in business telephony
- **Elderly User Considerations**: Account for Japan's aging population in voice AI evaluation criteria
